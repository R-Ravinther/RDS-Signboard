WHAT IS SIGNAGE

The word signage in Signs printing is very general and can be used to define everything from a finger sign on a post, an office nameplate, a way finding, a shop sign to some health and safety signs.

Shop signs can be made of anything from flat wood panels to aluminium trays, Fret cut letters fitted directly to the wall to light boxes, architectural letters in stainless steel to acrylic letters.
